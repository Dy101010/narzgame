on the higher levels of difficulty the computer opponents start out with more gold, higher wealth and more men. Here is a chart showing how much more of GMW (gold, men and wealth) each country starts out with on the higher levels of difficulty.

(1) Novice - 0% extra GMW
(2) Intermediate - 10% extra GMW
(3) Expert - 25% extra GMW
(4) Master - 50% extra GMW

I plan to make the harder levels more difficult in other ways as well, increasing AI intelligence and perhaps slowing down the speed in which the player can build up his economy (for example, maybe I'll make it cost 3 gold to raise the econ. level 1 point in the beginning).
