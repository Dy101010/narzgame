A couple weeks ago (June 19th, '02) I had an idea for a huge multiplayer tournament with six equal starts for six human players and one AI. On June 30th, the toruny began, not everything went quite according to plan (but then again it never does, does it?), there were seven human players, and only four of them the specificied "perfect" equal start. But nevertheless, this is going to be an exciting fight to the death! We will be meeting every Sunday in the early afternoon (usually between one and three PM, check the CivFanatic thread for exact time each week).

I have done write-ups of each week since the beginning, included is a brief summery (from my point of view) of how the game is going so far. Included (excpet in Week One) is some screenshots of the Top 5 city list and Powerchart. Of course this gives only a limited picture of whats going on, the top 5 cities are only five of many and the powerchart does not include many factors, such as economic strength, wonders of the world, diplomatic alliances or wars, etc. etc. Still, the commentary and the screenshots offer a glispe into how the game was going during that particular week.
