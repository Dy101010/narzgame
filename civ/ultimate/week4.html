Week Four - July 21st, '02

I cannot tell you too much about week four. I was at a business conference so my tribe (the Babylonians) was taken over by the evil AI. The game went on without me up to the year 25BC. I was sent the save file by GenSwartz so I could see how the AI manged my Civ and so I wouldn't have to take up too much time figuring out how to change everything when it came to be my turn. They really didn't do me as badly as I had expected. Besides abandoning my two-thirds completed wonder and not building enough new cities they did alright.

We should have a new player or two taking over for some abandoned Civs this coming Sunday. Though I'm sorry I missed it, I'm glad the game went on without me, I don't plan to miss any more!
