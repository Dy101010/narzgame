Week Seven - August 11th, '02

Five people turned out on this hot (in NY) Sunday. NRK, GenSwartz, Migonne and myself and FlowsLikeLava took over briefly for the Americans. The Romans and Germans were controled by the AI.

It was a quiet week on the battlefront, but I have a feeling it may be the calm before the storm, also the two main antagonists were not present :). The Sioux won the race to build Michaelangelo's Chapel, a key wonder. I (the Babylonians) had to be satisfied with Magellian's Expedition. NRK also built the Great Wall, an important and often underrated wonder, especially in a game where expansion is key.

The map is filling up, according to my embassies, each Civ now has at least nine cities. Things are about to get real interesting.
